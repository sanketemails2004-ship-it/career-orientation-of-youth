About the Project

What is Career Orientation?

Career orientation is a comprehensive process that helps young people understand themselves better, explore various career opportunities, and make informed decisions about their future. It combines self-assessment, career exploration, skill development, and practical guidance to prepare youth for successful transitions from education to employment.

  • Reduces anxiety and uncertainty about the future

  • Develops essential life skills beyond just career preparation
  • Builds professional networks early in career development
  • Enhances adaptability to changing job market conditions

How Career Assessments Work

Career assessments are scientifically designed tools that help you understand various aspects of your personality, interests, abilities, and values. By combining results from different assessment types, you can gain comprehensive insights into careers that align with who you are and what you value.
